Features

Custom POS Logo

Replace the default Odoo POS logo with your company logo or a custom image to match your store's branding directly on the POS interface.

Hide POS Logo Option

Option to completely hide the logo from your POS screen for a clean, distraction-free interface when preferred.

Custom Screen Saver

Set a personalized image or animated GIF as the POS screen saver, adding a professional and engaging touch when your POS is idle.

Screens

1. POS Logo & Screen Saver Configuration

  1. 1.1 Enable POS Logo

    Activate the POS Logo option to display a logo on the POS screen. This setting adds a professional branding touch to your POS interface, visible during customer transactions.

  2. 1.2 Logo Option

    Choose from three options for the POS logo: Company to display your company logo, Custom Image to upload a personalized logo image directly in the configuration, or No Logo to hide the logo entirely from the POS screen.

  3. 1.3 Enable Screen Saver Background

    Enable this setting to activate a custom screen saver background image or GIF that displays when the POS is idle. You can upload your preferred image or animated GIF within this option, adding a personalized, dynamic touch to your POS terminal.

  4. 1.4 Timer Color

    Define a custom color for the timer displayed on the POS screen saver. This allows you to match the timer's appearance with your brand's color scheme or screen saver design for a consistent visual experience.

2. POS Front End Screen

The POS front end screen displays your selected logo at the top of the interface during active sessions based on your chosen configuration: Company, Custom Image, or No Logo. Additionally, the custom screen saver background appears when the POS is idle, enhancing brand visibility and adding a polished touch to your POS environment.
